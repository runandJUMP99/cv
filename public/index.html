Jason Drees Coaching Platform

I assisted in the design and development of this web platform for a Coaching Company using AWS, Remix, and Zoho. The business owner is able to manage their many coaches, clients, engagements, invoices, etc. through Zoho, and each coach/client can manage their appointments, payments, onboarding, coaching forms, communication, etc. through the platform.

Made with AWS, React, and Remix

Photo Mules Desktop App

This desktop app was made using Electron and React for a photography company that took individual and group photos for large events. The owner was able to create users, assign admins, and all users were able to import, edit, and print photos. The app was synced locally between all other logged in users, with AWS when a connection was present, and with an external POS to stay current with new incoming orders.

Made with Electron, React, and AWS

Contacts 2 CRM

A small, easy to use mobile app made with React Native and AWS that allows users to connect to a CRM (Zoho, Salesforce, or Pipedrive) to quickly upload a contact. This was designed for users at large events that are trying to network and rapidly take someone's contact information. The user could scan a QR code, select from their local contacts, or manually enter a contact to upload with the ability to include default fields

Made with React Native and AWS

C Thru Inspections

A website build for a Home Inspection company. Users can easily view the services offered, associated fees, and easily contact the company or schedule an inspection on desktop, tablet, or smart phone devices. I also designed the logo and worked on the SEO for this client.

Made with React and Firebase

Random Draft League

A project for a streamer / content creator! Users can create accounts and contribue to each week's draft. The site has an admin section where the owner can edit and update the draft by selecting winners, sending notifications, and more!

Made with React, React-Redux, and Firebase

Cuppy's

A game where you work the closing shift for a new ice cream parlor, Cuppy’s, that has been having strange occurrences recently. Quickly assemble every customer’s favorite ice cream flavors and toppings before these… strange occurrences… get even worse!

Made with Unity
